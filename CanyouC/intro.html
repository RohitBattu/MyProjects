Welcome to QUEST 2K16 proudly presents " Can You 'C' "


It's a purely C-Programming-Language based contest-event. This event totally revolves around one single fact, "Coding is fun"! All the problems in the contest are totally meant to bring out the fun part of 'Coding' and test your knowledge in C-Programming-Language.

It's a go-to event for all the Code-Enthusiasts out there lurking in the wild, and for those fellow human beings who are yet to taste the sheer awesome-ness of coding, it's time you participate in our event.

This event, it is not designed like binary! One can be a noob and win the contest at the same time (That's the beauty of it)! The problems in the contest doesn't demand your advancement in technology, they revolve around logical-reasoning and basics of C-Programming-Language.

"Can You 'C'?" is an event in Quest, which is a National Level Technical Symposium organised by Department of CSE - JNTUH. Quest started in 2004 as a technical fest and right from its inception, it has received immense response from students all over the Telangana and AndhraPradesh. Quest offers a platform for students to compete with their peers in a myriad for students to events that test their mettle and knowledge in Computer Science and Engineering!
